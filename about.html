About EcoBITE
EcoBite is a web-based food ordering system designed for healthy, organic, and eco-friendly meals. Our goal is to make it easy for customers to browse products, add items to their cart, and place orders online while supporting sustainable dining.

Our Mission

Promote sustainable dining for a better experience.
